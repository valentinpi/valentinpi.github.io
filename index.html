About Me

I am a Computer Science student at the Freie Universität Berlin, currently beginning the second term of my Masters degree. I am further a student scientific assistant at the Fraunhofer AISEC, working on Quantum Algorithms for Cryptanalysis. I have a variety of interests in Computer Science and Mathematics. I also love History, Physics and Chemistry. Currently I am really keen on learning more about

  • Writing scientific papers (previously successfully did a dedicated course on it)
  • Graph Theory
  • Complexity Theory
  • Algorithmic Geometry (successfully did a dedicated course on it)
  • Quantum Computing and Quantum Mechanics
I am a member of the Internationale Arnim-Gesellschaft (IAG), a literary organization engaged in discovering the works by Achim von Arnim, one of the main representatives of the German Heidelberger "Junge" Romantik besides Clemens Brentano and Joseph von Eichendorff. He is known for his main work on German folk songs "Des Knaben Wunderhorn", his diverse tales and novels, as well as his expressive lyricism. Many rather unknown works of Arnim have not found the care and recognition they deserve yet, such as "Die Päpstin Johanna", "Ariel's Offenbarungen" and his dramas in the "Schaubühne" books. They are artifacts of a different time.
